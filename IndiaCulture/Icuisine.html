Welcome to Malaysian Indian Cuisine

Explore the rich flavors and vibrant dishes of India.

Introduction

Indian cuisine is known for its diverse flavors, vibrant spices, and rich history. From the tangy curries of the south to the hearty breads of the north, Indian food offers a variety of tastes for every palate.

Popular Dishes

Butter Chicken

A creamy and flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Biryani

A fragrant rice dish made with a blend of spices, meat, and sometimes vegetables, served with raita.

Samosa

A popular snack made of deep-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat.

Dosa

A South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter, cooked into a thin, crispy pancake, and filled with a spiced potato mixture. It’s served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Paneer Tikka

Cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture, then grilled or baked until golden. It's often served as a vegetarian appetizer with mint chutney.

Rogan Josh

A flavorful curry made with tender lamb or goat meat, cooked in a rich gravy of yogurt, spices, and herbs. It’s a staple of Kashmiri cuisine and is best enjoyed with rice or naan.
